# Supplier Incident Response SOP

Owner: Supply Chain Risk
Access Level: internal
Effective Date: 2026-01-01

Step 1. Triage

Classify the incident by severity, customer impact, affected materials, country, supplier, and expected recovery time. Incidents with customer impact or critical material risk must be escalated immediately.

Step 2. Impact Assessment

Quantify affected purchase orders, total quantity, due dates, inventory coverage, and alternate supply options. Use database facts for order exposure and keep model-generated recommendations separate.

Step 3. Mitigation Plan

Assign owners for expedite, safety stock review, alternate supplier activation, production rescheduling, and customer communication. Document assumptions and recovery dates.

Step 4. Management Review

Strategic supplier incidents and high-risk country disruptions require a management review. The review should include verified facts, open risks, mitigation status, and decision requests.

Step 5. Closure

Close the incident only after supply recovery is confirmed, customer impact is resolved, and corrective actions have evidence of completion.
