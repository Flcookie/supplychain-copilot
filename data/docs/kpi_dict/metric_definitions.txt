# Supply Chain KPI Dictionary

Owner: Supply Chain Analytics
Access Level: internal
Effective Date: 2026-01-01

Metric: on_time_rate
Business Name: On-time delivery rate (OTD)
Definition: On-time orders divided by total delivered orders.
Formula: SUM(CASE WHEN delivery_date <= due_date THEN 1 ELSE 0 END) / COUNT(purchase_orders.id)
Required Tables: suppliers, purchase_orders
Dimensions: supplier, country, material
Default Time Range: last_3_months
Minimum Sample Size: 10

Metric: order_volume
Business Name: Order volume
Definition: Purchase order count and ordered quantity.
Formula: COUNT(purchase_orders.id), SUM(qty)
Required Tables: suppliers, purchase_orders
Dimensions: supplier, country, material
Default Time Range: last_3_months
Minimum Sample Size: 1

Metric: avg_delay_days
Business Name: Average delay days
Definition: Average days late for delayed purchase orders only.
Formula: AVG(julianday(delivery_date) - julianday(due_date)) where delivery_date > due_date
Required Tables: suppliers, purchase_orders
Dimensions: supplier, country, material
Default Time Range: last_3_months
Minimum Sample Size: 5

Metric: defect_rate
Business Name: Quality defect rate
Definition: Defect count divided by delivered quantity.
Formula: defect_count / delivered_quantity
Required Tables: quality_events, delivery_lots
Status: Not available in the current demo SQLite database.

Metric: risk_score
Business Name: Supplier risk score
Definition: Weighted delivery risk, quality risk, audit risk, and disruption risk.
Status: Not available in the current demo SQLite database; requires enterprise risk tables.
